VPN


In what follows, we briefly describe the configuration details:

  1. The OpenVPN server runs on the UDP port 1194
  2. Register your Self for VPN.
  3. Download your VPN keys.
  4. See the quick openvpn client setup guide for ubuntu . You can Further Check out the OpenVPN howto for details on how to setup and start an OpenVPN client on your Windows, Linux or Mac laptops.
  5. On successful connection the client will be automatically assigned an IP addess in the range 10.8.0.x with routes set to the Baadal internal VLANs. Your default route will not be altered from what has been set to connect to your ISP.
    The VPN connection will be point-to-point and the broadcast traffic of the 10.8.0.x VLAN will not be available to the client.
